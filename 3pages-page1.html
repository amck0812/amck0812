Page One - Kitty

Say hello to my cat!

This is my cat. Her name is Kitty. I've had her since she was a kitten, and she's about 8 years old now.
She's a brown tabby with medium-length fur and big yellow-green eyes.

She is somewhat skittish and easily-startled, kind of like me. She's kind of weird in that she prefers kibble over wet food.
She's really talkative too. She's shy at first but super sweet when she gets to know you.

I love Kitty a lot. She has been there for me through rough periods in my life, and I know that she could never judge me.
I'm glad to have such a cute and sweet cat!
