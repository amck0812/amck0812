Page Two - Dot

Now say hello to my OTHER cat!

This is Kitty's son, Dot. Dot was a mysterious litter of only one. He was born with a black dot on his head, thus the name,
but it disappeared as he aged. Like his mom, he has yellow-green eyes.

Dot is a completely fearless cat who only listens to you when he feels like it. When he's not annoying dogs 10x his size,
he's begging for another meal. His appetite is insatiable.

Ever since I watched Dot be born and watched him grow firsthand, I knew he'd be a very interesting cat.
I'm glad that he's both so friendly and so fearless.
